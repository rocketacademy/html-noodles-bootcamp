Fish Ball Noodles

Ingredients for one servings

  • 2 packets of fresh fishballs
  • 1 piece of fried fishcake
  • 5 pieces of fish dumplings
  • 1 tbsp of dong cai
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 300g noodles

    • Instructions

    1. Marinate minced pork for at least 15 minutes
    2. In a pot, bring the ikan bilis broth to boil. Add dongcai, fishball, fish dumplings and sugar into pot
    3. Allow to simmer until fishballs are cooked, and floating on surface of broth.
    4. Bring a large pot of water to boil, and add noodles
    5. Add noodles to bowl of cooked fishballs!
    6. Serve hot and enjoy!
