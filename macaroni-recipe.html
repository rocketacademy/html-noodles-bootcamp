Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation time:

20 minutes

Cooking time:

25 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Whisk in the flour until smooth and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring constantly, until the mixture is smooth.
  6. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and well combined.
  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to combine.
  8. Pour the macaroni and cheese mixture into a greased baking dish.
  9. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the top of the macaroni and cheese.
  10. Bake for 25 minutes, until the breadcrumbs are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
