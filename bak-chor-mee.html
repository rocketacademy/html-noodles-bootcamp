Bak Chor Mee


Ingredients

Ingredients
Yellow Noodles
Chinese Meatballs and Fishballs
Crispy Fried Lard
Crispy Fried Shallots
Chinese Black Vinegar

Preparation time

4hrs 30mins

Instructions

  1. Marinate all the meat mince, meat slices and liver.

  2. Soak the mushrooms overnight, then cook them for an hour the next day.

  3. Next Day: We make the soup from scratch by boiling the meat bones for just over and hour, with some aromatics.

  4. Make the sauce for the noodles with some homemade or shop bought sambal paste, liquid from the mushrooms, some soup and a few other ingredients. This takes 2 minutes.

  5. Blanch the noodles and beansprouts
  6. At the same time, we cook the meatballs and fishballs in the soup.

  7. We cook the mince, liver and meat slices in the same soup while the balls are cooking.

  8. We mix the sauce, noodles and beansprouts in individual bowls.

  9. Top with all the meat and the balls.

  10. Ladle some soup over (optional).

  11. Top with stewed mushrooms and liquid.

  12. Final garnish.
