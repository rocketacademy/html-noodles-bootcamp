Quick Creamy Chicken & Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 can Campbellâ€™s Condensed Cream of Chicken and Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 3 cups cooked egg noodles

Preparation Time

  • 25 minutes

Instructions

  1. Heat the soup, milk, cheese, chicken and noodles in a 4-quart saucepan over medium heat until the mixture is hot, stirring occasionally.
  2. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve sprinkled with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
