Green Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • Thai basil
  • Bamboo shoots, canned
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green curry paste, store bought or homemade
  • Makrut lime leaves
  • Full fat coconut milk
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar (sub granulated or light brown sugar)
  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Chicken stock, unsalted

Preparation Time: 60 Minutes

Instructions:

  1. Pound green curry paste and Thai basil leaves for an extra vibrant colour. Get the paste as fine as possible.
  2. Reduce the coconut milk until it has a thick consistency
  3. Add the curry paste and saute for a few minutes.
  4. Toss chicken with the curry paste.
  5. Add coconut milk
  6. Add chicken stock
  7. Add makrut lime leaves.
  8. Add fish sauce and palm sugar.
  9. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is fork tender.
  10. Add bamboo shoots and cook for a minute until the curry comes back to a boil.
  11. Stir in red bell pepper and turn off the heat.
  12. Add Thai basil and stir just until wilted.
  13. Serve the green curry with jasmine rice
