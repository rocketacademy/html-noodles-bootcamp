Chicken Noodle Soup

Recipe Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • ½ pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • ½ teaspoon dried basilh
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
Preparation & Cooking Time
30 minutes

Instructions
  1. In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter. Cook onion and celery in butter until just tender, 5 minutes.
  2. Pour in chicken and vegetable broths and stir in chicken, noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes before serving.
