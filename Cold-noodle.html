Cold Chicken Noodle Salad Recipe

Cold chicken noodles salad with chicken and soba noodles. Perfect for a hot day, this recipe is delicious, cooling, and easy to make.

Click to return to main recipe page

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

INGRDIENTS

  • 1 skinless and boneless chicken thigh or leg meat, cut into small pieces
  • 200g egg noodles
  • 1.5 tablespoonb soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 6 pieces of diced spring onions

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the chicken leg and once done, Shred the meat from the chicken leg, and discard the bones. Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the noodles following pack instructions. Drain the noodles and cool in cold running water.

  2. Mix together the soy sauce, sesame oil, lemon juice and sugar until the sugar has dissolved.

  3. Toss the cooled noodles with the dressing and arrange on a serving plate. Top with the chicken, chilli, spring onions and coriander. Toss everything through just before serving.
