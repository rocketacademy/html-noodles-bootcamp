Onion Egg Recipe

Ingredients:

    Main Ingredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups (1 can) chicken broth
    • Seasonings:
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Preparation time:

10minutes

Instructions

  • Cut the onion in half and cut it into thin slices.
  • Crack the eggs into a bowl and add in the seasoning (1 tsp soy sauce, 1 tsp white pepper and 1/4 tsp salt).
  • Beat the eggs. Set aside.
  • Heat the wok/pan with 1 tbsp cooking oil.
  • Put the slices onion in the wok/pan and fry it under medium high heat for around 2 minutes. Put 1/4 tsp of salt to soften the onions and enhance it taste.
  • Put the fried onion to the eggs mixture. Mix well.
  • Put the onions and egg mixture back to the wok/pan. Fry around 90 seconds by using the medium low heat.
  • Flip to another side and fry another 90 seconds by using the medium low heat.
