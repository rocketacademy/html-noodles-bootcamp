Chicken Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • Seared seasoned chicken thigh fillets (or breast)
  • 1 canned, pureed tomato sauce
  • cooking oil
  • 4x 6 inches flour tortillas

Preparation time

  • Filling a quesadilla: 10 minutes
  • Cooking a quesadilla: 10 minutes

Instructions

Filling a quesadilla:

  1. To fill a quesadilla, place the filling on one side of a tortilla, then fold to enclose.
  2. Start with shredded cheese, top with filling of choice (beef, chicken, vegetarian) then finish with another sprinkle of cheese.

    Cooking a quesadilla

  3. Place the quesadilla in a dry skillet over low to medium low heat.
  4. Cover with a lid – this heats the filling through and melts the cheese faster, before the tortilla gets too brown
  5. Flip over the folded edge -this ensures you don’t lose any filling! Use your hand on the uncooked side to hold it together.
  6. Cook uncovered until crispy and cheese is molten – about 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Cut in half and serve!
