Recipe for Shanghai Spring Onion Oil Noodles

Ingredients

  • 400g dried thin, round noodles
  • 5 stalk spring onion, cut into long sections
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar

Prep: 2 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Total: 12 mins

Instructions

  1. Pour oil in a wok. Add spring onion. Leave to simmer over a medium low heat until it starts to brown. Take the onion out and set aside (see note).
  2. Add soy sauce and sugar to the oil. Cook until the sauce starts to bubble. Turn off the heat.
  3. Place noodles in the wok. Stir well to evenly coat the noodles with the sauce.
  4. Portion out the noodles into 4 serving bowls. Top with fried spring onion.
