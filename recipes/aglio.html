Aglio Olio

Preparation Time: 30mins

Ingredients

  1. Spaghetti or pasta of your choice: 400 grams
  2. Fresh garlic: 4-6 cloves (sliced thinly)
  3. Extra virgin olive oil: 1/2 cup
  4. Red chili flakes: 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  5. Fresh parsley: 1/2 cup (finely chopped)
  6. Salt: to taste
  7. Freshly ground black pepper: to taste
  8. Grated Parmesan cheese (optional): for serving

Instructions

  1. Boil Pasta: Start by boiling a large pot of salted water. Once boiling, add the spaghetti and cook until al dente (usually about 8-10 minutes, but check the package instructions). Reserve about a cup of the pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare Garlic and Oil: While the pasta is cooking, thinly slice the garlic. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic is just golden, not browned (this should take about 2-3 minutes).
  3. Combine Pasta and Garlic Oil: Drain the pasta and add it directly to the pan with the garlic oil. Toss well to coat the pasta in the garlic oil. If the pasta seems dry, add a little of the reserved pasta water to help emulsify the sauce.
  4. Season and Serve: Add the chopped parsley, and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Toss everything together well.
  5. Optional: Serve with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
  6. Enjoy: Your Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is ready to enjoy!
