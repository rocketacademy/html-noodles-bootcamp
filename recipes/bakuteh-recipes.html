Ba Ku Teh Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 cups of water
  • 2 Tbsp ground white pepper
  • 1 Tbsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 whole star anise
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 20 garlic cloves
  • 6 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 lbs pork ribs, rinsed and drained, cut into 4 pieces
  • cooked rice, for serving

Preparation Time:

2 Hour 10 Minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add all the ingredients, except the prok ribs, and bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Add the pork ribs to the broth and bring back to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer on low for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the pork is tender.
  3. Turn off the heat and cover for 10 minutes. Serve hot with rice.
