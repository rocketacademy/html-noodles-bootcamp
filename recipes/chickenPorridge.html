Chicken Porridge

Preparation Time: 30mins

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of rice (preferably short-grain or jasmine)
  2. 6 cups of chicken broth or water
  3. 2 chicken breasts (skinless, boneless)
  4. Salt, to taste
  5. Ginger, a 2-inch piece (sliced)
  6. Green onions, a few stalks (chopped)
  7. Optional toppings: soy sauce, sesame oil, fried garlic, boiled eggs, chopped cilantro, or pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse the Rice: Wash the rice in cold water until the water runs clear. This step removes excess starch and prevents the porridge from becoming too thick.
  2. Cook the Chicken: In a pot, add the chicken breasts, ginger slices, and 6 cups of chicken broth or water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to simmer. Cook the chicken for about 20 minutes or until fully cooked. Remove the chicken, shred it into pieces, and set aside. Keep the broth.
  3. Cook the Porridge: In the same pot with the broth, add the rinsed rice. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to a low simmer. Cook for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. If the porridge is too thick, you can add more water or broth to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Season the Porridge: Once the rice is soft and the porridge has a creamy texture, add salt to taste.
  5. Serve: Ladle the porridge into bowls, top with shredded chicken, and add your desired toppings like green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, fried garlic, boiled eggs, cilantro, or pepper.
  6. Green onions, a few stalks (chopped)
  7. Enjoy: Serve hot and enjoy your comforting bowl of chicken porridge!

This recipe can be easily customized to your taste, and you can add more ingredients like vegetables or different proteins to make it more substantial. It's a great dish for breakfast or a light meal.
