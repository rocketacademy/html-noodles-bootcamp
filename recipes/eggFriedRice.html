Egg Fried Rice

Preparation Time: 30mins

Ingredients

  1. Cooked rice: 2 cups (best if left overnight in the fridge)
  2. Eggs: 2-3, beaten
  3. Green onions: 3, finely chopped
  4. Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  5. Vegetables (like peas, carrots, bell peppers): 1 cup, diced
  6. Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
  7. Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon
  8. Salt: to taste
  9. Pepper: to taste
  10. Vegetable oil: for cooking

Instructions

  1. Prep Ingredients: Ensure your rice is cooked and ideally chilled (leftover rice works best). Chop the green onions, mince the garlic, and dice any additional vegetables you're using.
  2. Cook the Eggs: Heat a little vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until just set. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
  3. Sauté Vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds. Then add the other diced vegetables and cook for a few minutes until they're just tender.
  4. Add Rice: Increase the heat to medium-high and add the cooked rice. Stir-fry the rice with the vegetables, breaking up any clumps.
  5. Season the Rice: Add the soy sauce and sesame oil. Stir everything together and cook for another minute or two. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  6. Add Cooked Eggs: Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan. Add the green onions. Stir everything together gently to combine.
  7. Serve: Serve the egg fried rice hot, straight from the wok.
  8. Enjoy: Your Egg Fried Rice is ready to enjoy!
