Lemongrass Chicken with Yakisoba Noodles

Ingredients for 1 serving
Chicken
  • 6.5oz Chicken Thighs

  • 1/2 tablespoon Lemongrass, minced

  • 3/4 teaspoon Soy Sauce

  • 3/4 teaspoon Brown Sugar

Noodles
  • 1/4 package Yakisoba Noodles

  • 1/2 tablespoon Canola Oil

  • 1/2 tablespoon Soy Sauce

  • 1/2 tablespoon Fish Sauce

  • to taste Carrots, shredded

Dipping Sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon Fish Sauce

  • 1/2 teaspoon Rice Vinegar

  • 3/4 teaspoon Granulated Sugar

  • 34 teaspoon Lemon Juice

  • 3/4 teaspoon Water

Garnish
  • to taste Cucumbers, sliced

  • to taste Scallions

  • to taste Jalapeno Peppers, sliced

  • to taste Fresh Cilantro

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Instructions:
  1. Marinade the Chicken Thighs (6.5 oz) in Lemongrass (1/2 Tbsp) , Soy Sauce (3/4 tsp) , and Brown Sugar (3/4 tsp) for about 30 minutes to an hour.


  2. Heat up Canola Oil (1/2 Tbsp) in a wok. Stir fry Yakisoba Noodles (1/4 pckg) . Add Soy Sauce (1/2 Tbsp) and Fish Sauce (1/2 Tbsp) . Stir well but be careful not to break up the noodles. Add Carrots (to taste) . Set aside.


  3. Stir fry the chicken until fully cooked.


  4. You can add the noodles back into the wok or serve the chicken and the noodles separately.


  5. Mix Fish Sauce (1/2 tsp) , Rice Vinegar (1/2 tsp) , Granulated Sugar (3/4 tsp) , Lemon Juice (3/4 tsp) , and Water (3/4 tsp) for the dipping sauce. Serve the sauce on the side.


  6. Garnish with Cucumbers (to taste) , Scallions (to taste) , Jalapeño Peppers (to taste) , and Fresh Cilantro (to taste) .
