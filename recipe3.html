Recipe Title

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 3 onions (320g), finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely grated
  • 125g celery, chopped

Preparation Time:

40 minutes

Instructions:

  1. STEP 1 Heat the oil in a large pan over a medium heat and fry the onions and garlic for 5 mins, then add the celery and peppers. Fry for another 5 mins, adding the smoked paprika in the last minute. Stir in the tomatoes, oregano and stock. Bring to the boil.
  2. Tip in the penne, green beans and kale, bring back to the boil and cook over a medium heat for 10 mins. Stir in the cherry tomatoes and basil, and cook for a few minutes more until the tomatoes have burst.
  3. STEP 3 To serve, spoon two portions of the soup into shallow bowls and sprinkle over half the cheese. Keep the remainder for another day. Will keep chilled in an airtight container for up to four days or frozen for up to three months. Reheat in a pan over a low-medium heat until piping hot, then serve with the remaining cheese.
