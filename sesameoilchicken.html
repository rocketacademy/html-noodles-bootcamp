Rasa Malaysia's sesame oil chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2-3 inches (5 cm-7 cm) ginger knob, peeled and cut into thin strips
  • 12 oz. (340 g) chicken, cut into pieces
  • ½ package of tofu (optional), sliced into ½ inch thick bite size pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine or Chinese rice wine
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 dashes ground white pepper

Preparation

Preparation time: 20 minutes

  1. Heat up a skillet or wok over high heat and add the sesame oil.
  2. When the oil is fully heated, add the ginger strips and stir fry until light brown or aromatic.
  3. Add the chicken and stir fry for about 10-15 seconds before adding the soy sauce, wine, and ground white pepper.
  4. Add the water and do a few quick stirs.
  5. Cover the skillet or wok with the lid and turn the heat to low heat and let the chicken cook for about 10 minutes or until the sauce thickens and the meat becomes tender.
  6. Dish out and serve immediately.
