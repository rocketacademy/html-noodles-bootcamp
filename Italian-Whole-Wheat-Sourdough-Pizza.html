INGREDIENTS

DOUGH

  • Sourdough starter
  • 150g whole wheat flour
  • 120g water
  • 2g sea salt

TOPPINGS

  • Sourdough starteWhatever veggies you like! Besides those listed here, eggplant, onion, spinach, and zucchini are a few other tasty options.
  • Tomato puree/store-bought pizza sauce, 1/4 cup or to taste
  • 1/2 pack of oyster mushroom (~50g)
  • 1/2 a bell pepper, sliced
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Bring a large pot (5-quart capacity or greater) of heavily salted water to boil. Meanwhile, prepare your kale by slicing or stripping the leaves from the stems (save them). Discard the rough bottom of the stems, then slice the stems into pieces about Â¼-inch wide. Place the stem pieces in a fine-mesh sieve.
  2. Mix all dough ingredients and set aside to rise, until it expands roughly 1.5 times in volume (depending on how much starter you used, and how hot the weather is, it can take 3-6 hours).
  3. Shape the dough into a ball, and store it in a covered bowl/container in the fridge overnight, or up to 3 days, till you are ready to assemble the pizza.
  4. Take out the dough ball ~30 minutes before assembling, and let it come to room temperature; meanwhile, chop the veggies.
  5. On a baking tray lined with parchment paper or pizza stone, drizzle a few drops of olive oil and spread it evenly across - this prevents the dough from sticking and makes it much easier to handle.
  6. Place the dough ball at the centre of the baking tray/pizza stone, and start to press it outwards (watch this video for an illustration); then stretch it until you reach the desired shaped and thickness (I prefer it thin).
  7. Spread the tomato puree or pizza sauce evenly - be careful not to use too much as it might make the pizza overly sour.
  8. Add the veggie toppings (except any fresh herbs).
  9. Bake at 250 degrees Celsius for 15-20 minutes, till the veggies and dough are cooked through (you will be able to see a distinct colour change).
  10. Top with fresh herbs and enjoy!
