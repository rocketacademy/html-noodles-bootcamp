MaPo Tou Fu

Preparation Time: 20 Minutes

Description

Mapo tofu is a popular Chinese dish from Sichuan province known for its fiery bright red sauce.

The dish is spicy, aromatic, deeply flavorful, and has a unique numbing character imparted from the Sichuan peppercorns.

    Ingredients

  • Ground pork - turkey, chicken, or beef can be used
  • Soft tofu
  • Aromatics - Green onions, ginger, garlic
  • Garlic, ginger, and scallion
  • Doubanjiang (spicy/non-spicy broad bean)
  • Oyster sauce
  • Light soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Potato starch

    Instructions

  1. Prepare all the ingredients.
  2. Cook the aromatics and ground pork in a frying pan or wok.
  3. Add the sauce ingredients and let it simmer until the sauce starts bubbling.
  4. Add the tofu and coat it with the sauce until the flavors infuse.
  5. Add the chicken back to the pan and toss it.
  6. Serve on its own or with steamed rice!
