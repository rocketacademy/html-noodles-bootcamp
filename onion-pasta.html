Onion Pasta Noodles

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 5 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 1 pound uncooked pasta, cooked according to package directions

YIELDS

2 - 4

TOTAL TIME

20 MINS

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a large skillet place oil, butter and onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add basil, pepper, water and bouillon and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook noodles following the instructions on the package. Drain then briefly rinse under running water.
  4. Add onion mixture to cooked pasta, sprinkle with cheese, toss and serve.
