Har Cheong Gai

  • Chicken mid wings
  • Shrimp Sauce

2h

  • 01 - In a large mixing bowl, add chicken wings, shrimp sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, sesame oil, Chinese wine, white pepper and salt. Stir to combine, ensuring each wing is coated evenly, then cover in cling film and allow to rest in the fridge for up to 2 days.
  • 02 - In another large mixing bowl, combine plain flour, potato starch, bicarbonate of soda, baking powder, eggs and water. Whisk until fully combined, the mixture should be runny but thick enough to hold on the surface of a spoon.
  • 03 - Add oil to a wok over medium heat and bring up to temperature.
  • 04 - Once the wings have had time to marinade, remove from the fridge then dip in batter. Place the battered wings in the oil in batches of 4 and cook for 3-4 minutes until the coating crisps up and turns a golden brown.
