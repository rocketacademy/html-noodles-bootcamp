Mee Soto Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, divide into 4
  • 4 onions
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 inch galangal
  • 8 candlenuts
  • 3 lemongrass, crushed
  • 5 pandan leaves
  • 4 tablespoon soto or soup mix
  • 1 stick of cinnamon
  • 2 chicken cube (optional)
  • 4 litres water
  • 1 kg yellow noodles
  • 200g beansprouts
  • 1/2 cup spring onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsely
  • Fried shallots

For the chilli condiment:

  • 1/4 cup bird eye chillies
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3/4 cup sweet soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions

Preparation Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

For the chilli condiment:

  1. Blend all of the ingredients until fine.

For the soup:

  1. Blend onion, garlic, ginger, galangal and candlenuts until a fine paste.
  2. Add some oil in a pot and saute the blended ingredients with lemongrass and cinnamon.
  3. Add in the soto/soup mix and cook for about 5 minutes
  4. Add the chicken and continue cooking for another 5 minutes
  5. Pour in water and add the pandan leaves and chicken cubes.
  6. Season with salt and cook for about 20-30 minutes until the chicken is very tender.
  7. Remove chicken from the soup and tear chicken into strips. Set aside.
  8. Stain the soup to remove the lemongrass, pandan leaves and cinnamon.

To serve:

  1. In a small pot of boiling water, blanch the yellow noodles until soft. Strain and place in bowl.
  2. Blanch the beansprouts for 15 seconds, drain and top it on the noodles.
  3. Top the noodles with the soup and chicken strips.
  4. Garnish with spring onions, parsley, fried onions, with the chilli condiment on the side.
