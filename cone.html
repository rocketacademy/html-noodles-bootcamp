Pizza Cone

Preparation time: 60 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Plain flour 2 cups
  • Dry yeast 1 teaspoon
  • Dry milk powder 2 teaspoons
  • Sugar 1 teaspoon
  • Salt ¼ teaspoon
  • Egg 1
  • Oil 2 teaspoons
  • Warm water as required

Instructions:

  1. Combine dry ingredients, mix in egg, oil, and water. Rest dough for 45 minutes to double in size.
  2. Create cone templates with paper, transfer to card paper, wrap with foil.
  3. Roll out dough, cut into cone shapes using templates.
  4. Mold dough around cones, seal edges. Bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Remove cones from oven, let cool. Remove card paper cones.
  6. In a bowl, mix cooked chicken, pizza sauce, capsicum, onion, green chilies, and cheese.
  7. Fill cones with mixture, top with cheese and oregano.
  8. Bake for 5 minutes until cheese melts. Wrap bottom of cones with butter paper and serve.
