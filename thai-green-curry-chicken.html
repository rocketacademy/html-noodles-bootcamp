Thai Green Chicken

Ingredients

  1. ¼ pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
  2. ¾ teaspoon dark soy sauce
  3. ¾ teaspoon all-purpose flour
  4. ½ tablespoon cooking oil
  5. ½ tablespoon green curry paste
  6. ½ green onions with tops, chopped
  7. ¾ cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  8. ¼ teaspoon fresh ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  9. ½ cup coconut milk
  10. ¾ teaspoon fish sauce
  11. ¾ teaspoon dark soy sauce
  12. ½ tablespoon white sugar
  13. 2 tablespoons cilantro leaves, for garnish

Preparation time

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 40 mins
  • Total: 1 hr
  • Servings: 1
  • Yield: 1 servings

Instructions

Step 1

Toss chicken first in 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce, then in the flour, coating pieces evenly. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Place chicken in the skillet, cook and stir chicken until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken.

Step 2

Reduce heat to medium and stir in curry paste. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant, then stir in green onions, garlic, and ginger; cook an additional 2 minutes. Return chicken to the skillet, stirring to coat with the curry mixture. Stir the coconut milk, fish sauce, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, and sugar into the chicken-curry mixture. Allow to simmer over medium heat for 20 minutes until the chicken is tender. Serve garnished with cilantro leaves.
