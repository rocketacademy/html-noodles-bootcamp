Pig stomach soup

Home

A healthy boiled soup with various pork innards and meats. It is rich in protein.

Preparation time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • Pig stomach
  • Pork balls
  • Pork liver
  • Mushrooms
  • Tofu

Steps

Step Procedure
1 Ensure the pig stomach is washed cleaned. Use cooking vinegar to soak if necessary.
2 Throw all the ingredients into a pot and boil for 2 hours.
3 When the soup starts smelling good, you know it's time to serve!
