Keto Avocado Popsicles

Ingredients:

  • 2 avocados
  • 1/2 cup macadamia nut milk or nut milk of choice
  • 1/3 cup allulose syrup or 2-3 tablespoons powdered erythritol, plus 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Add the peeled and seeded avocados to a blender or food processor.
  2. Pour in nut milk, allulose syrup (or powdererd erythritol and vanilla extract if using as a replacement) and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into popsicles molds. Add popsicles sticks and freeze overnight or until hardened.
