Hor Fun

Ingredients

  • 400 g Thick Rice Noodles Hor Fun
  • >150 g Pork slices
  • 12 pcs Prawns devein
  • 5 cloves Garlic minced
  • 5 stalks Bok Choy
  • 1 qty Egg

Preparation time

It takes at most 1 hour to prepare this dish.

Instructions

  1. Mix sauces together
  2. Heat pan/wok till slightly smoky
  3. Add oil in then add Hor Fun noodles and add in 1 tsp of soy sauce. fry until brown on the edges
  4. Remove the noodles
  5. In the same pan add cooking oil add pork in.
  6. Add chopped garlic then the prawns
  7. Add in the chicken stock, bring to boil.
  8. Add Bok Choy and stir in the corn starch solution.
  9. Let it simmer for approximately 2 minutes
  10. Add cooked hor fun and mix with the gravy and serve!
