Laksa

Ingredients

THE BROTH:

  • 4 lemongrass , bruised and cut into 1 inch (2.5 cm) length
  • 4 garlic cloves , grated
  • 2 thumb sized ginger , grated
  • ⅓ cup Thai red curry paste (90 gr)
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper powder (optional)
  • ⅓ cup water (80 ml)
  • 3 cup less sodium fish/seafood/chicken stock (750 ml)
  • 1 chicken breast , thinly sliced
  • 12 shrimps , peeled
  • 3 tablespoon coconut sugar
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 1 cup coconut milk (250 ml)

THE NOODLES AND CONDIMENTS

  • 2 oz dry rice vermicelli noodles (60 gr)
  • 5 oz dry egg noodles (140 gr)
  • some firm tofu
  • 2 eggs
  • some coriander leaves
  • sambal

Preparation

THE BROTH:

  1. Heat some oil in a pot on medium heat. Add in lemongrass, garlic and ginger. Cook until garlic and ginger turn golden brown.
  2. Add in red curry paste, cayenne pepper powder and water. Mix well and continue cooking for 1 minute. Then add in fish stock and chicken. Stir and leave to simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Finally add in shrimps, coconut milk, sugar, lime juice and stir everything together. Turn the heat off when the shrimps are fully cooked.

THE CONDIMENTS:

  1. While the broth is cooking, prepare the condiments. Shallow fry tofu, slice and set aside. Make 6 ½ minutes eggs, peel and set aside. Cook noodles according to package instructions, set aside.

HOW TO SERVE:

  1. Place some cooked noodles in a bowl. Ladle in some broth (discard the lemongrass pieces) together with chicken pieces and shrimps.
  2. Place half an egg, some sliced fried tofu, coriander leaves, sambal and lime wedges. Serve hot, enjoy!
