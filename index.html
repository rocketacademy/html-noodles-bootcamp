Simple Food Ideas with 5 Ingredients (or Less!) Recipes

Tortellini with Sausage and Mascarpone

Recipe Link

Ingredients

1 package (20 oz) refrigerated cheese tortellini
8 oz bulk Italian sausage
1 jar (24 oz) pasta sauce with mushrooms
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1 carton (8 oz) mascarpone cheese
Crushed red pepper flakes, optional

Instructions

1. Prepare tortellini according to package directions.
2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink, 6-8 minutes, breaking into crumbles; drain. Stir in pasta sauce; heat through.
3. Drain tortellini, reserving 1 cup cooking water. Add tortellini to sauce with enough reserved cooking water to reach desired consistency; toss to coat. Stir in Parmesan cheese; dollop with mascarpone cheese. If desired, sprinkle with red pepper flakes.

Nutrition Facts

1 cup: 637 calories, 37g fat (17g saturated fat), 113mg cholesterol, 1040mg sodium, 57g carbohydrate (11g sugars, 4g fiber), 24g protein.

One-Pot Mac and Cheese

Recipe Link

Ingredients

3-1/2 cups whole milk
3 cups water
1 package (16 oz) elbow macaroni
4 oz Velveeta, cubed
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper

Instructions

1. In a Dutch oven, combine milk, water and macaroni; bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat and simmer until macaroni is tender and almost all the cooking liquid has been absorbed, 12-15 minutes, stirring frequently. Reduce heat to low; stir in cheeses until melted. Season with salt and pepper.

Nutrition Facts

1 cup: 637 calories, 37g fat (17g saturated fat), 113mg cholesterol, 1040mg sodium, 57g carbohydrate (11g sugars, 4g fiber), 24g protein.

Easy Peanut Butter Balls

Recipe Link

Ingredients

1 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup peanut butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 to 5 cups cornflakes

Instructions

1. In a large saucepan, bring the corn syrup and sugar to a boil. Add the peanut butter. Remove from the heat; stir in vanilla and cornflakes. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper. Store in an airtight container.

Nutrition Facts

1 peanut butter ball: 69 calories, 3g fat (1g saturated fat), 0 cholesterol, 44mg sodium, 11g carbohydrate (9g sugars, 0 fiber), 1g protein.

Devil’s Food Cookies

Recipe Link

Ingredients

1 package devil's food cake mix (regular size)
2 large eggs, room temperature
2 tablespoons butter, softened
3 tablespoons water
1/2 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°. In a large bowl, combine cake mix, eggs, butter and water (batter will be thick). Fold in chocolate chips. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto baking sheets coated with cooking spray. Bake 10-13 minutes or until set and edges are lightly browned. Cool 2 minutes before removing to wire racks.

Nutrition Facts

1 each: 105 calories, 4g fat (2g saturated fat), 17mg cholesterol, 155mg sodium, 16g carbohydrate (9g sugars, 1g fiber), 1g protein.
