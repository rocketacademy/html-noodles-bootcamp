Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Preparation Time

10 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mash the avocados with a fork or potato masher until they are mostly smooth.
  2. Stir in the onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, cumin, and cayenne pepper until well combined.
  3. Taste and adjust seasonings as necessary.
  4. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
