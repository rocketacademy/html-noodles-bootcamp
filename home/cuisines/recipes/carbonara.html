Carbonara

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese

20mins

  • Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  • 02 - Finely chop the 100g pancetta, having first removed any rind. Finely grate 50g pecorino cheese and 50g parmesan and mix them together.
  • 03 - Beat the 3 large eggs in a medium bowl and season with a little freshly grated black pepper. Set everything aside.
  • 04 - Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add 350g spaghetti and when the water comes back to the boil, cook at a constant simmer, covered, for 10 minutes or until al dente (just cooked).
  • 05 - Squash 2 peeled plump garlic cloves with the blade of a knife, just to bruise it.
