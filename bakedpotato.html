Baked Potato

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 8 large baking potatoes
  • 300ml pot soured cream

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan.
  2. Rub a little oil over each potato and put on the top shelf of the oven.
  3. Bake for 20 mins, then turn down the oven to 190C/170C fan and bake for 45 mins-1 hr more until the skin is crisp and the inside soft.
  4. Make a cross in the centre of each potato, dollop over the soured cream and serve.
