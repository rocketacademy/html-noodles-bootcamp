Steamed Fish with Ginger and Scallions

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole fish, about 1 pound (such as sea bass, tilapia, or trout)
  • 3-4 slices ginger
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine or sherry
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Cilantro leaves, for garnish

Preparation time:

20 minutes

Cooking time:

15 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Clean the fish and pat it dry with paper towels. Make a few slits on both sides of the fish.
  2. Place the ginger slices and half of the scallions on a heatproof plate that fits inside a steamer basket. Place the fish on top of the ginger and scallions.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, soy sauce, rice wine or sherry, sugar, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Pour the mixture over the fish.
  4. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Place the steamer basket over the pot and steam the fish for 12-15 minutes, until cooked through.
  5. Carefully remove the plate from the steamer basket and discard the ginger and scallions.
  6. Garnish the fish with the remaining scallions and cilantro leaves.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
