1. Curry Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Prepartion Time:

25 Minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, salt, and cayenne pepper.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook until translucent, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the spice mixture to the skillet and stir to coat the chicken.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and coconut milk to the skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let cook until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened, about 15-20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro if desired.
