Classic Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation time:

10 minutes

Cooking time:

N/A

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the romaine lettuce with the dressing until well coated.
  3. Add the croutons and grated Parmesan cheese to the bowl and toss again.
  4. Serve immediately.
