Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Total Time: 55 minutes

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Thighs
  • 5 Slices of ginger

Marinde

  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp ShaoXing wine

Herbs

  • 5g Dang Gui
  • 5 Red dates - halved
  • 10g Dang shen - 2 pieces
  • 10g Yu Zhu
  • 2 tsp Gei Qi

Others

  • 1 piece of parchement or cooking paper

Instructions

  1. Remove Remove fats from chicken thighs and rinse. Marinade chicken thighs for at least an hour.
  2. Place a sheet of parchment paper on a heat-proof plate. Place half the herbs on top of the parchment paper followed by the chicken legs. Then scatter the remaining herbs on the chicken and pour the marinade sauce over them. (If you want some sauce with the chicken, add 2 to 3 tbsp of water.)
  3. Fold the paper over the chicken lengthwise and seal the sides. Alternatively, you can also tie it up into a parcel.
  4. Fill a wok/steamer with enough water to steam. Steam over medium high heat for about 45 minutes. Check occasionally to ensure there is enough water. If the water dries up too much, top up with some water. Serve hot.
