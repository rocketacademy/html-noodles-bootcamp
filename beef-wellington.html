Beef wellington

Ingredients

  • 2 x 400g beef fillets
  • Olive oil, for frying
  • 500g mixture of wild mushrooms, cleaned
  • 1 thyme sprig, leaves only
  • 500g puff pastry
  • 8 slices of Parma ham
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten with 1 tbsp water and a pinch of salt
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation time

  • 20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Place the frozen puff pastry on the counter so that it will have time to thaw
  2. Afterward, prep the onion, mushrooms, and egg
  3. Place the beef tenderloin in a small baking dish or pan, and spread it with two tablespoons of softened butter
  4. Roast the tenderloin in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes or until browned
