Sangria recipe


Ingredient list

  1. 1 (750-ml.) bottle red wine
  2. 3/4 c. fresh orange juice
  3. 1/2 c. brandy
  4. 1/4 c. granulated sugar
  5. 1 orange, sliced
  6. 1 apple, sliced
  7. 1 c. fresh blueberries
  8. 1 c. sliced fresh strawberries
  9. 1 (3") cinnamon stick
  10. 100 MB foo
  11. 640 pixels bar

Preparation time: 15 min

Refrigeration time: 2 hours

Instructions

  1. In a large pitcher, mix wine, orange juice, brandy, and granulated sugar. Stir in oranges, apples, blueberries, foo, bar, strawberries, and cinnamon.
  2. Refrigerate until ready to serve, at least 2 hours or preferably overnight.
