Mie Ayam Recipes


Preparation Time ‚è±: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 300g fresh egg noodles, boiled
  • 6 tablespoons of oil
  • 3 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • 2 bundles of bok choi, blanched

For the chicken topping:

  • 250g chicken, cubed
  • 1 leek
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 lemongrass, crushed
  • 6 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of white pepper
  • 250mL water

Instructions:

  1. Boil the chicken with all the ingredients for the topping
  2. Drain the chicken and cut into cubes. Save the rest of the liquid for the noodles.
  3. Heat up oil, add the vegetables and the noodles
  4. Add back some of the sauce from boiling the chicken.
  5. Toss all ingredients together. Serve while hot.
