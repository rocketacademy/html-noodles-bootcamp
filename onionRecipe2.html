Onion Pasta

Pasta noodles and grilled onions, generously garnished with grated parmesan cheese.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil/li>
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 5 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp chicken stock boullion
  • 1 lb uncooked pasta, cooked according to package directions

Preparation Time

  • Preparation: 10 min
  • Cooking: 15 min
  • Total Time: 25 min

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet place oil, butter and onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add basil, pepper, water and bouillon and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Add onion mixture to cooked pasta, sprinkle with cheese, toss and serve.
