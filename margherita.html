Pizza Margherita

Preparation time: 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 medium carrot, finely diced
  • 2 ounces pancetta, thickly sliced, finely diced
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1 (28-ounce) can peeled Italian tomatoes, seeded and finely chopped, juices reserved
  • 1 cup chicken stock or canned low-sodium broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 pounds penne rigate
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Pizza Dough: Whisk together flour, sugar, yeast, salt. Add water and olive oil, stir until combined.
  2. Knead the dough for 3 minutes until smooth and slightly tacky. Place in a greased bowl and let rise for 2 hours.
  3. Preheat Oven and Pizza Steel/Stone: Preheat oven and stone to 550°F (285°C) for at least 1 hour.
  4. Assemble Ingredients: Prepare tomato sauce, cheese, basil, and parmigiano-reggiano cheese.
  5. Separate and rest the dough for 5-10 minutes. Stretch one ball into a 10-inch circle.
  6. Transfer dough to pizza peel or baking sheet. Brush with olive oil, add sauce, cheese, basil.
  7. Slide the pizza onto the heated stone and bake for 7-8 minutes until crust is golden.
  8. Remove pizza from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, cheese, and basil. Slice and serve.
