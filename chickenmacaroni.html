Chicken macaroni noodle soup

Ingredients

Serves two

  • 60g macaroni
  • 50g shiitake mushrooms, minced
  • 220g of chicken breast on bone
  • 50g lean minced pork
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 a cup of fried shallots
  • 15ml cooking oil
  • 4 cups of water

Preparation

Preparation time: 45 minutes

  1. Separate chicken breast meat from bone.
  2. Cook chicken bone in the 4 cups of water for 45 minutes.
  3. Cook the macaroni in salted water according to packet instructions. Drain.
  4. Salt the chicken breast. Steam and shred.
  5. While chicken breast steams, heat up the oil on medium high heat in a wok. Stir fry the garlic, then add the minced shiitake mushrooms. Once fragrant, add the minced pork.
  6. Once the minced pork is browned, add the bone stock to the wok.
  7. Place the macaroni in bowls (for servings for each person). Add the broth, shredded steamed chicken breast and top with the browned mince and mushrooms. Serve topped with fried shallots.
