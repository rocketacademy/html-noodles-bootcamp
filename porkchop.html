Pork Chop

Recipe title:

Honey garlic pork chops

Ingredients:

  1. 4 pork chops bone in or out
  2. Salt and pepper, to season
  3. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  6. 6 cloves garlic, minced
  7. 1/4 cup honey
  8. 1/4 cup water (or chicken broth)
  9. 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar (or apple cider vinegar, or any white vinegar)

Preparation time:

  • Prep Time: 1 Hour
  • Cook Time: 1 Hour
  • Total Time: 2 Hours

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven broiler (or grill) on medium-high heat. Season chops with salt, pepper and garlic powder just before cooking.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or skillet over medium high heat until hot.
  3. Sear chops on both sides until golden and cooked through (about 4-5 minutes each side).
  4. Transfer to a plate; set aside.
  5. Reduce heat to medium. Melt butter in the same pan, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  6. Saut√© garlic until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  7. Add the honey, water and vinegar. Increase heat to medium-high and continue to cook until the sauce reduces down and thickens slightly (about 3-4 minutes), while stirring occasionally.
  8. Add pork back into the pan, baste generously with the sauce and broil/grill for 1-2 minutes, or until edges are slightly charred.
  9. Garnish with parsley and serve over vegetables, rice, pasta or with a salad.
