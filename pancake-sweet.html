Total time to prepare: 40 mins

    Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fine salt / 1/4 teaspoon(if using salted butter)
  • Milk
  • Melted Butter
  • One Egg
  • Vanilla Extract

    Instuctions

  1. Play "Be Ok" on repeat
  2. Whisk dry ingredients together
  3. Melt butter and warm milk (we use the microwave)
  4. Whisk wet ingredients together
  5. Lightly spray the waffle iron with nonstick cooking spray, if desired.
  6. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  7. Cook on a hot buttered skillet
  8. Enjoy!
