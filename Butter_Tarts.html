Butter Tarts

Ingredients:

  • Pastry shells
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • raisins
  • pecans (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Place the pastry shells on a baking sheet.
  4. Put raisins or pecans in each shell (optional), then fill with the butter mixture.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the filling is set.
  6. Let cool before serving.
