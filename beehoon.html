Bee Hoon

    Recipes

    Ingredients

    • 160g Raw dried beehoon
    • 65g Bean sprouts
    • 1tbsp Light soya sauce
    • 1/2tbsp Dark soya sauce
    • 1/2tbsp Fish sauce
    • 1/2tsp Sugar
    • 1tsp Chicken powder

    Instructions

    1. Blanch beehoon in hot boiling water to soften texture, quickly drain and dry, set aside.
    2. In a mixing bowl, combine, light soya sauce, dark soya sauce, fish sauce and chicken powder. Mix well and set aside
    3. In a pan, saut√© garlic till fragrant, add in beehoon and stir fry till its well mixed.
    4. Add in bean sprouts, continue to mix well.
    5. Pour in sauce mixture and toss well to well combined. Add a little bit of water to help mix easier.
    6. Dish up and serve hot!
