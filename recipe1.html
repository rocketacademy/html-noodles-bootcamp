Beef & Onion Soy Sauce Noodles

Ingredients for 1 serving
  • 4.5 oz Beef

  • 1/4 teaspoon Corn Starch

  • 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce

  • 1 1/8 teaspoon Dark Soy Sauce

  • 3/4 teaspoon Granulated Sugar

  • 1/4 Small Onion

  • 4 oz Spaghetti

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Instructions:
  1. Follow the directions of the Spaghetti (4 oz) package for al dente pasta, but subtract 1-2 minutes from what they suggest. We will be cooking this again in a frying pan so we want to under-cook the pasta.


  2. Once the noodles are done, pour in into a strainer and lightly rinse off the extra starch and set aside.


  3. Cut the Onion (1/4) in half, then and slice them into thin half moon slices and set aside.


  4. Combine the Dark Soy Sauce (1 1/8 tsp) , Soy Sauce (1 Tbsp) , and Granulated Sugar (3/4 tsp) into 1 bowl and set aside.


  5. Cut the Beef (4.5 oz) thinly against the grain and put it into a bowl.


  6. Marinate the beef with 2-3 teaspoons of the soy sauce mixture from step 2 and Corn Starch (1/4 tsp) for 5-10 minutes.


  7. In a frying pan (preferably a non-stick), add 2-3 tablespoons of oil and set it to high heat and wait for it to get hot.


  8. Add in the onions and cook it for 3 minutes until soft and golden.


  9. Add in the beef and cook for 3-5 minutes until it looks done.


  10. Add in the spaghettini and then add the sauce mixture on top of everything and cook for 2-3 minutes until everything is nice and brown and evenly mixed. Serve and enjoy!
