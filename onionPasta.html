Onion Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 5 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 1 pound uncooked pasta, cooked according to package directions

Preparation Time

Approximately 10-15 minutes of preparation time.

Instructions

Step 1: In a large skillet place oil, butter and onions and cook until golden brown.

Step 2: Add basil, pepper, water and bouillon and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.

Step 3: Add onion mixture to cooked pasta, sprinkle with cheese, toss and serve.
