Homemade Chicken Soup

Chicken Soup Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you'll need for this chicken soup recipe:

  • Chicken: 3-pound whole chicken.
  • Vegetables: Carrots, celery, and an onion.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, & chicken bouillon granules

Cooking Details

Here are the cooking details for this recipe:

Prep Time Cook Time Servings
15 mins 1 hr 30 mins 10

Instructions

Follow these steps to whip up a good meal!

  1. Bring chicken, water, and 1 tablespoon salt to a boil in a large stockpot.
  2. Skim foam. Add onions, celery, and garlic. Reduce heat. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken breast, and set aside. Add carrots. Simmer for 40 minutes.
  4. Discard back and wing. Cool slightly. Remove meat from bones. Cut into bite-size pieces.
  5. Stir in desired amount of chicken. Skim fat. Season with salt.
