Ipoh Shredded Chicken Noodles

Prep time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) shrimp meat
  • 400g (0.8 lb) chicken breast
  • 1kg (2 lbs) flat rice noodles (Hor Fun)
  • 1 Cup Bean Sprouts
  • 100 g (4 oz) chives, cut into 3 cm segments
  • 3 stalks of scallions, cut into short sections
  • 3 stalks of chives
  • 3 fresh red chilies, sliced
  • 4 tbsp crispy fried shallots

Soup Base

  • 1.4 kg (3lbs) chicken bone
  • Sufficient water to submerge the chicken
  • 1 Tbsp Whole White Peppercorns
  • 30g (1 oz) rock Sugar
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 500g (1 lb) Shrimp Shells
  • 100ml (3.5 oz) cooking oil ( for frying the shrimp heads)

Instructions

Chicken and shrimp broth

  1. Pace the chicken bones in a pot with cold water enough to cover all the bones. Skim away the scum floating on the surface. Add the peppercorn. Simmer over low heat for one hour.
  2. Stir-fry the shrimp heads until fragrant and turn golden, which takes about 15 to 20 minutes. Strain the shrimp heads and keep the excess shrimp oil separately.
  3. Add the fried shrimp heads to the chicken broth and simmer over low heat for 20 minutes.
  4. Strain the broth with a strainer or cloth bag. Season with rock sugar and salt.

Others

  1. Simmer the chicken breast meat in the clean stock until it is cooked, which takes about 15 minutes.
  2. Shred to become chicken floss.
  3. Poach the shrimp in the broth. Set aside.
  4. Slice the shallots thinly. Deep-fried over low heat until crispy and brown. Pour it through a wire mesh strainer to remove the excessive oil.

To assemble

  1. Blanch the noodles, bean sprouts, and chives separately in hot water for 20 seconds.
  2. Put the hot hor fun in a large bowl, top with the shredded chicken, bean sprouts, shrimps, fried shallots, chives, red chili, and scallions. Ladle the hot stock over the noodles. Add a teaspoon of the fragrant shrimp oil and serve immediately.
