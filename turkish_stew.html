Turkish Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 Â½ cups dry couscous
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound tilapia fillets, cut into chunks
  • ground black pepper to taste

Prep Time:

15 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring 3 cups water to a boil, and stir in the couscous. Remove from heat, cover, and let sit 5 minutes.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, and mix in the fish chunks. Reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes, or until the fish is easily flaked with a fork. Serve over couscous.
