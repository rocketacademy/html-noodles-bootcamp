Nissin Cup Noodles - Chicken Flavour

Preparation time: 1 min.

Ingredients

  • Nissin Cup Noodles - Chicken Flavour
  • Hot water

Instructions

  1. Open lid
  2. Carefully remove satchets inside.
  3. Pour contents of sachets provided into the cup.
  4. Heat water.
  5. Add hot water to cup noodles.
  6. Wait 3 minutes.
  7. Enjoy.
