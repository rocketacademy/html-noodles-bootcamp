Schnitzel

Ingredients

  • Veal
  • Breadcrumps
  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil

Preparation time

  • 20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Pound the veal cutlet to a slice about ¼ inch thick
  2. Coat the meat in flour, then in beaten eggs, and lastly in breadcrumbs.
  3. Fry it in a pan in a thick layer of fat (clarified butter is ideal) on both sides.
  4. Put the finished Wiener Schnitzel on paper towels in order to drain off the excess fat.
  5. Don’t press the meat when coating it in breadcrumbs, just dust it a little.
  6. Fry the Schnitzel in a pan.
