Chicken Mami Recipe

<< Back to Main Page

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 12 oz chicken breast (bone in)
  • 1 pc chicken boullion cube
  • 1 lb fresh mami noodles
  • 2 cups cabbages, chopped
  • 3 eggs, boiled
  • 1 cup carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup green onion, chopped
  • 1 head onion, wedged
  • 1 head garlic, crushed and chopped
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1/2 cooking oil
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine chicken breast, onion.and 2 quarts water. Let the water boil.
  2. Add chicken boullion cube. Continue to boil for 40 minutes between low to medium heat.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the toasted garlic. Combine crushed garlic and cooking oil in a small pan or pot. Turn the heat on and cook slowly while stirring until garlic browns. Filter the garlic using a kitchen sieve. Note: Save the oil to cook other dishes
  4. Heat 1 quart water in a small pot. Let boil. Add fresh noodles. Cook for 1 minute. Discard water. Arrange noodles in serving bowls, including Napa cabbage, carrot, and egg.
  5. Once the chicken cooks completely, remove from the pot. Separate the meat from the bone and let it cool down. Slice the chicken into serving pieces.
  6. Arrange the noodles, vegetable, and chicken in a serving bowl. Pour hot broth and top with chopped green onions and toasted garlic. Serve. Share and enjoy!
