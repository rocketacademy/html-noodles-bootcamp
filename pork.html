Chinese Pork Dumplings

Pork Dumplings Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you'll need for this delicious recipe:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped Chinese chives
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • ½ cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

Cooking Details

Here are the cooking details for this recipe:

Prep Time Cook Time Servings
20 mins 60 mins 5

Instructions

Follow these steps to whip up a good meal!

  1. Combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, chives, sesame seeds, and chile sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix pork, garlic, egg, chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger until thoroughly combined.
  3. Place a dumpling wrapper on a lightly floured work surface and spoon 1 tablespoon of the filling in the middle.
  4. Wet the edge with water and form small pleats to seal the dumpling. Repeat.
  5. Heat 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Place 8 to 10 dumplings in the pan and cook until browned, about 2 minutes per side.
  7. Pour in 1 cup of water; cover and cook till tender and pork is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  8. Repeat to cook remaining dumplings, adding more oil and water for each batch.
  9. Serve dumplings with dipping sauce.
