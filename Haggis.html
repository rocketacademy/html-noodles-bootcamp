Haggis

Ingredients:

  • Sheep's heart, liver, and lungs
  • onion
  • oatmeal
  • suet
  • spices
  • stock

Instructions:

  1. Clean the sheep's heart, liver, and lungs.
  2. Boil them with onion until cooked, then mince the meats.
  3. Mix the minced meats with toasted oatmeal, suet, and spices.
  4. Stuff the mixture into a sheep's stomach or an artificial casing.
  5. Boil the haggis in stock for several hours.
  6. Serve with neeps (turnips) and tatties (potatoes).
