Banmian

Home

A soup noodle dish that you can find at most hawker centres and coffee shops in Singapore. It is made from a meat or seafood based stock, with flour noodles and served alongside hearty ingredients like vegetables, minced pork balls and egg.

Preparation time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • Ikan bilis
  • Fresh vegetables
  • Minced pork
  • Noodles
  • Egg

Steps

Step Procedure
1 Put the ikan bilis and pork in water and bring to a boil
2 Add noodles, then vegetables midway, and cook
3 When almost ready, crack the egg into the pot and serve
