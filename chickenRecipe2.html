Creamy Chicken Noodles

Noodles and chicken are coated in a creamy, savory sauce- it's a great use for leftover chicken or rotisserie chicken. Quick, family-friendly, and no-stress to nake.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 1 can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups cubed and cooked chicken
  • 3 cups cooked egg noodles

Preparation Time

  • Preparation: 15 min
  • Cooking: 10 min
  • Total Time: 25 min

Instructions

  1. Heat the soup, milk, cheese, chicken and noodles in a 4-quart saucepan over medium heat until the mixture is hot, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add soy sauce and sugar to the oil. Cook until the sauce starts to bubble. Turn off the heat.
  3. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve sprinkled with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
