Hokkien Noodle

Ingredients

  • 400 grams thick egg noodles
  • 1 cup pork belly sliced
  • ¾ cup large shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup Napa cabbage thinly sliced
  • 1 cup gai lan chopped
  • ¼ cup chicken stock unsalted
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • ½ tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1.5 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp kecap manis aka sweet soy sauce
  • ½ tbsp white granulated sugar
  • ½ tbsp cornstarch
  • ½ cup water

Preparation Time

30mins

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine Sauce ingredients as listed above. Set aside.
  2. In a wok filled halfway with water, bring to a boil. Blanch your noodles for only 20-30 seconds or until loosened. Strain immediately. Any longer and your noodles will be soggy.
  3. Dry your wok and over medium heat, add oil and pork belly. Fry until the pork belly has released some fat and is browned on the edges.
  4. Toss in garlic and shrimp. Fry until shrimp is 50% cooked.
  5. Increase heat to medium high. Add noodles and Sauce. Mix the sauce into noodles allowing the sauce to reduce a bit, about 45-60 seconds.
  6. Then add cabbage, gai-lan and chicken stock. Toss everything together and cook until there's a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of the wok. Remove off heat and enjoy!
