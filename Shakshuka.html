Shakshuka

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 can (28 ounces) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Preparation Time:

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Pour in the can of whole peeled tomatoes with their juices and use a wooden spoon to break them into smaller pieces. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the sauce has thickened, about 10 minutes.
  4. Using a spoon, make 6 small wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cover the skillet and simmer until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny, about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro and serve hot with crusty bread or pita.
