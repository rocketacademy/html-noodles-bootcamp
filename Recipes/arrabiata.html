Spaghetti Arrabiata

Credits
  • Recipe from Recipetineats
  • Image from TasteAtlas
  • Serves: 4-5 people
  • Calories: 530 kcal
  • Total Cooking Time: 35 mins

Ingredients

  1. 500 g dried spaghetti
  2. 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  3. 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  4. 21 oz / 600g crushed tinned tomatoes
  5. 2 tbsp chilli flakes
  6. 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Directions

  1. Cook 500g of dried spaghetti (or any other long dried pasta) in a large pot of salted boiling water per packet MINUS 1 minute.
  2. Heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic, stir until fragrant, then add the tinned tomatoes and chilli flakes, simmer on medium for 5 minutes.
  3. When pasta is ready, scoop out 1 cup of pasta cooking water, and drain pasta.
  4. Add pasta into chosen sauce with 3/4 cup pasta cooking water. Toss pasta until sauce thickens and sticks to pasta (called "emulsifying" the sauce), 1 - 2 minutes. Use more pasta water if needed to loosen sauce.
  5. Toss through parsley, serve with parmesan. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
