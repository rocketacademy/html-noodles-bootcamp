Turmeric Curry Chicken

Credits
  • Recipe from Nomadette
  • Image from Resepi Che Nom
  • Serves: 2 people
  • Calories: 456 kcal
  • Total Cooking Time: 40 mins

Ingredients

    Chicken

  1. 300 grams of Chicken
  2. 2 Cups Water/Chicken Stock
  3. 200 ml of Coconut Milk
  4. 1 tbsp of Salt to taste
  5. 1 tsp of Sugar
  6. 4-5 Kaffir Lime Leaves

    7. Base paste

  7. 1 Whole Red Onion
  8. 3-4 cloves of Garlic
  9. 1 inch fresh turmeric, or 1 tsp of turmeric powder
  10. 1 Lemongrass stalk, white inner core, sliced
  11. 10 Thai Bird’s Eye Chilies or Cili Padi
  12. 2-3 tbsps of Water for easier blending

Directions

  1. Roughly chop the ingredients for the base paste for easier blending. Add all the ingredients in the blender, along with water, and blend together to create a paste.
  2. Heat pan, and add in the blended paste. Stir in water or stock.
  3. Add in chicken next, and let the soup come to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, turn the heat down to a simmer. Put a lid on and let the pan simmer for about 15 to 20 minutes, or until the chicken has cooked through. Add more water if it might have evaporated.
  5. Still on medium-low heat, gently stir in coconut milk, and season with salt and sugar to taste.
  6. Tear in kaffir lime leaves. Dish is done and ready to be served with rice.
