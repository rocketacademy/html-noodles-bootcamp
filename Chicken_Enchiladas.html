Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 can (15 oz) red enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Preparation Time

20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken, onion, sour cream, cilantro, and salt.
  3. Warm the tortillas in the microwave for 20 seconds or until they are pliable.
  4. Pour half of the enchilada sauce into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Divide the chicken mixture evenly among the tortillas, and roll them up tightly. Place them seam-side down in the baking dish.
  6. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top of the tortillas, and sprinkle with the shredded cheese.
  7. Cover the dish with foil, and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil, and bake for an additional 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
