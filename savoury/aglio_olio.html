Aglo e Olio

    Ingredients
  • 250g dried spaghetti (or any other long pasta)
    60g extra virgin olive oil
    5 garlic cloves, sliced
    1/2 tbsp red chilli flakes
    chopped parsley, personal preference
      • Full cooking time: 20mins
      Instructions
  • 1) Cook dried pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water as per packaging minus 1 minute.
    2) Heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook until golden - be careful not to burn it.
    3) When pasta is ready, scoop out 1 cup of pasta cooking water, and drain pasta.
    4) Toss pasta with sauce and pasta cooking water. Toss pasta until sauce thickens and sticks to pasta (about 1-2 minutes).
    5) Toss through parsley and chilli flakes, season with salt and pepper to taste.
