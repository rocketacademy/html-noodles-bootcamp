Indonesian Dishes

Indonesian cuisine is a collection of various regional culinary traditions that formed the archipelagic nation of Indonesia.
Basic ingredients of Indonesian cuisine consist of a variety of herbs, seasoning, and spices.
Most Indonesian dishes use fresh herbs such as onion and garlic, spring onion, ginger roots, turmeric, galangal, candlenuts, lemon basil, lemon grass, and not to mention chilli.
