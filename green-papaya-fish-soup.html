Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 39 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fish head
  • 1 small green papaya
  • 4 dried red dates
  • 60g ginger
  • 1 tsp wolfberry seeds
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 5 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash the fish clean. If using a fish head, make sure that the cavity is thoroughly cleaned. Chop/cut into big pieces (I get the fishmonger to cut the fish head). Pat dry with paper towels and rub some salt on all sides of the fish. Set aside.
  2. Bring water to boil in a soup pot.
  3. While waiting for the water to boil, peel the papaya with a vegetable peeler. Remove all the seeds and cut it into bite-sized chunks.
  4. Heat up sesame oil in a separate frying pan/pot. Fry half of the ginger for about a minute before adding the fish to fry. Brown the fish on both sides until it is completely cooked.
  5. When the water starts to boil, put in the red dates, fried fish, green papaya chunks and the rest of the ginger into a claypot or any suitable soup pot. Avoid stirring the soup to prevent the fish from breaking. Let it simmer for about 30 minutes.
  6. Season with salt if necessary. Optional: Pour â…› tsp of sesame oil into the soup if desired. Serve hot.
