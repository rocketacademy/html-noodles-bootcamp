Cranachan

Ingredients:

  • Fresh raspberries
  • honey
  • oatmeal
  • whisky
  • double cream

Instructions:

  1. Toast the oatmeal until golden brown.
  2. Whip the double cream until stiff, then stir in honey and whisky.
  3. Gently fold in the toasted oatmeal.
  4. Layer the cream mixture and fresh raspberries in glasses.
  5. Chill before serving.
